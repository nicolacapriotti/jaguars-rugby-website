Join the Jaguars Rugby Club

No experience required. Whether you're a high school athlete looking for a new challenge or an adult missing the competitive spirit of team sports, the Jaguars Rugby Club welcomes you.

High School players from Junction City, Abilene, Chapman and surrounding communities are encouraged to join. Our Men's team welcomes players of all experience levels who want to compete, train hard, and build brotherhood through rugby.
