About the Jaguars

Our mission is to foster an environment where men may learn, grow and succeed through hard work, sportsmanship and fellowship. We accomplish this by teaching the sport of Rugby. We believe that the values and traditions of this sport are vital to the development of men today. We aspire to not only develop these men into great rugby players, but great leaders and members of society.

High School Rugby Club

The Jaguars High School Rugby team is a club sport welcoming players from Junction City, Abilene, Chapman and surrounding communities. Because rugby operates as a club program, athletes from multiple schools are able to participate together.

Our program introduces players to the fundamentals of rugby while emphasizing teamwork, leadership, discipline and physical fitness. Many athletes begin with no rugby experience and quickly develop into confident competitors while building strong friendships with teammates.

Men's Rugby Club

The Jaguars Men's Rugby Club provides an opportunity for adults in the Junction City area to continue competing in rugby while building strong friendships through sport.

Our club welcomes experienced rugby players, former athletes from other sports, and beginners interested in learning the game. The team focuses on competition, fitness, and building a strong brotherhood within the community.

Our Core Values

Brotherhood

We believe that by teaching brotherhood we instill in our players a sense of service. With this sense of service these people learn to contribute and demonstrate that care, compassion and dedication to each other and their community will lead to enduring relationships both within the organization and the larger community around them.

Integrity

We believe that honesty and forthrightness is vital to learning how to communicate and interact with the people in and around our lives. We hold ourselves to the standard of doing the right thing even when no one is around. We strive to say what we mean and do what we say.

Hustle

We believe in the value of hard work, determination and perseverance. This value transcends sports.
