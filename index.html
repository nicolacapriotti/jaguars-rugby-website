About the Jaguars

The Junction City Jaguars Rugby Club is a competitive rugby program based in Junction City, Kansas. Our club includes both a high school team and a men's team focused on developing players, building brotherhood, and growing the sport of rugby in our community.

Our mission is to foster an environment where men may learn, grow and succeed through hard work, sportsmanship and fellowship. We accomplish this by teaching the sport of Rugby.
